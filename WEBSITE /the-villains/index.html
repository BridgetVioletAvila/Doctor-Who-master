The Villains Homepage

The Daleks first appeared in the second Doctor Who story, they were the first monsters to face the Doctor in the TV series. The Cybermen appeared a few years later in the 1st Doctors final story. The Master is a rival Time Lord who only cares about causing destruction in the universe. Other popular enemies include the Sontarans, Ice Warriors and Silurians. The Sontarans are a clone race bred for war, they are one of the few aliens to infiltrate Gallifrey. The Ice Warriors were originally a warrior race from Mars however in the 50th century they became respected members of a Galactic Federation. The Silurians originally inhabited the Earth, before man. The Silurians are not a violent species but many of their species want to reclaim the Earth as their own.
